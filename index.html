Kandama

The kendama (けん玉, "sword and ball") is a traditional Japanese skill toy.

Component

The Kendama consisted by three main different parts which are handle (ken), a ball (tama) and use string to connect handle and ball. The entire Kendama has three cups and on hole on the ball

How to play

The modern playing style influences from the wide range of technique such as yo-yo, juggling and diabolo.
